Frequently Asked Questions

What is GoodWire?

For more information about who we are as a company. Visit our about page for more details.

How long will it take for my order to ship?

All orders will be shipped within 1-3 business days of being placed. If you have any questions about your order, please visit our contact page and fill out the form.

What is your return policy?

All orders are subject to a 30-day return period and 90-day exchange perod. To submit one of these requests please visit our contact page and fill out the form. Please note that all returns and exchanges may be subject to an additional fee if the supplier requires it. All returns and exchanges must be in the original packaging and in the same condition as when it was received.

What is your warranty policy?

All products ordered will come with the standard 1-year warranty. This covers all manufacture defects and does not cover accidental damage. If you require this extended coverage, you can purchase it at checkout.

How do I track my order?

All orders can be tracked using our Order Tracking page. This page will give you convienent access to order tracking. If you are a GoodWire member, you can access the tracking for each of your orders directly on your profile.
